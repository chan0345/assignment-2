Welcome!

Swimming is a great way to get your daily dose of exercise. It is recommended to have at least 1 hour of exercise every day. What's a better way to get that into your system than by having fun in the refreshing water!

Our facility provides the public with various types of pools, such as a Swirl Pool, Shallow Pool, 25m Pool, 50m Pool, and a Kiddie Pool.
